what i learnt:

Through this experiment, I learned that:

1. Prompt Quality Directly Affects Output Quality

A better prompt leads to better responses. AI performs significantly better when instructions are clear and detailed.

2. Context Matters

Providing context helps the model understand the goal and generate more relevant results.

3. Structure Improves Clarity

Well-structured prompts produce responses that are easier to understand and better organized.

4. Specificity Produces Better Visuals

When visual expectations are clearly defined, image generation becomes more meaningful and polished.

5. Prompt Engineering is an Important Skill

Writing effective prompts is not just asking questions — it involves giving the AI proper guidance, intent, and constraints.

Final Conclusion

This experiment showed that:

Better prompts = Better AI outputs

A simple prompt can work, but a carefully engineered prompt produces significantly higher-quality results in terms of explanation, examples, structure, and overall presentation.

Prompt engineering is becoming an essential skill for effectively using modern AI tools.
