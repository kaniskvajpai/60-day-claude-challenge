# Startup

**Name:** Farm2Fresh AI (Working Title)

**Industry:** Food Processing & AgriTech

**Problem Statement**

Small and medium food processing businesses struggle with inefficient inventory management, inconsistent quality control, manual production planning, and demand forecasting. These issues increase operational costs, reduce profits, and lead to significant food wastage.

# Customer Insights

- Customers care more about reducing waste than adding automation.
- AI forecasting provides immediate business value.
- Simplicity is preferred over feature-heavy software.
- Mobile dashboard access is highly requested.

---

# MVP Recommendations

✔ AI Demand Forecasting

✔ Inventory Management

✔ Waste Analytics

✔ Production Planning

✔ Reporting Dashboard

---

# Key Learnings

- Customer interviews are essential before product development.
- Focus on solving one critical pain point first.
- Build a lightweight MVP to validate assumptions.
- Pricing should align with customer ROI.
- Continuous feedback drives product-market fit.
