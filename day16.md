# Day 16 – Building a Custom Claude Skill for Stock Fundamental Research

## Objective

For Day 16 of the **#60DayClaudeChallenge**, I created a reusable Claude Custom Skill called **stock-fundamental-research** to automate comprehensive fundamental stock analysis.

## What I Built

* Created a Custom Skill: **stock-fundamental-research**
* Added a detailed description and reusable instructions
* Saved the skill for future use
* Tested it using **Adani Enterprises**

## Stock Analyzed

**Company:** Adani Enterprises

The generated report included:

* Company overview
* Business model and diversified operations
* Revenue and profitability analysis
* Financial ratios
* Valuation metrics
* Shareholding pattern
* Institutional ownership trends
* Growth drivers
* Key business risks
* Investment outlook

## What Surprised Me

The most surprising insight was how the AI connected multiple aspects of the business into a single structured report. Rather than simply listing financial ratios, it explained how Adani Enterprises' diversified business model, expansion plans, ownership trends, and valuation interact to influence its long-term investment outlook.

The Custom Skill also maintained a consistent report format, making it easy to compare different companies without rewriting prompts each time.

## Key Learnings

* Custom Skills eliminate repetitive prompting.
* Consistent prompt engineering produces more reliable outputs.
* AI can significantly speed up investment research.
* Human verification remains essential before making any investment decisions.
* Reusable AI workflows improve productivity for financial analysis.
