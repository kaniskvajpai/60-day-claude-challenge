# Key Learnings

### 1. Context Matters
Providing detailed context significantly improves AI responses.

### 2. Structured Prompts Work Better
Clear objectives produce more actionable outputs.

### 3. AI as a Workflow Partner
Claude is most effective when used throughout a project lifecycle rather than for isolated questions.

### 4. Usage Monitoring Improves Efficiency
Tracking interactions helps optimize productivity and avoid unnecessary prompts.

---

# Challenges

### Challenge
Getting generic responses.

### Solution
