# Day 33 – Marketing Strategy Simulator

## #60DayClaudeChallenge

**Date:** 12 July 2026

---

# Objective

Developed an interactive **Marketing Strategy Simulator** using Claude AI to help users create data-driven marketing strategies through audience analysis, platform selection, content planning, and growth reporting.

---
# Project Overview

The simulator enables users to:

* Select Business, Personal Brand, or Random Client mode
* Identify and analyze the target audience
* Choose the most suitable marketing platforms
* Create three strategic content pillars
* Generate a personalized 30-day marketing roadmap
* Respond to simulated marketing challenges
* Review a final Growth Report with recommendations

---

# Key Learnings

* Audience research should always come before content creation.
* Different platforms serve different marketing objectives.
* Content pillars improve consistency and long-term brand growth.
* AI can assist in strategic planning, not just content generation.
* Marketing decisions should be supported by data and continuous experimentation.
