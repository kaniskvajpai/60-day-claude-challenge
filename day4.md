# Day 4 — Career Roadmap & Strategic Planning

## 🎯 Day 4 Goals

The objective of Day 4 was to create a structured career roadmap by reflecting on long-term goals, current strengths, skill gaps, and actionable milestones. The exercise focused on building clarity around career direction and identifying measurable next steps for growth.

I am currently exploring career opportunities in data science while building technical and problem-solving skills. My goal is to work in a role that combines **technology, innovation, and impact**, with long-term aspirations toward leadership and strategic decision-making.

### Current Gaps

### Immediate (0–3 Months)

### Mid-Term (3–6 Months)

✅ Develop portfolio-quality projects

✅ Apply for internships/opportunities

# 🛣 Personalized Career Roadmap

## Phase 1: Foundation Building (0–3 Months)

**Goal:** Strengthen core knowledge and consistency
**Milestones:**

* Complete learning roadmap
* Build 1–2 mini projects
* Improve technical fundamentals

---

## Phase 2: Portfolio & Experience (3–6 Months)

**Goal:** Gain practical experience
**Milestones:**

* Create strong GitHub portfolio
* Build resume-worthy projects
* Participate in competitions/hackathons

---

## Phase 3: Career Readiness (6–12 Months)

**Goal:** Prepare for professional opportunities
**Milestones:**

* Internship/job applications
* Personal branding (LinkedIn/GitHub growth)
* Industry networking

# 💡 Reflection — Biggest Insight

The biggest realization from reviewing this roadmap is that career growth is not about achieving everything at once — it is about building consistent momentum through small, measurable actions. Breaking larger goals into timelines makes progress feel more realistic and actionable.
