# Day 34 – Marketing Detective 🔎📈

## 60 Day Claude AI Challenge

### Objective
Built an interactive **Marketing Detective** application using Claude that teaches marketing strategy through detective-style investigations. The application presents randomized campaign failure cases where users analyze evidence, identify marketing mistakes, and learn best practices.

---


# Solved Case Report

### Case

Declining conversion rate despite increasing advertising budget.

### Evidence Collected

- CTR was high.
- Landing page bounce rate exceeded 82%.
- Mobile loading time was 7+ seconds.
- Product messaging differed between ads and landing page.
- Audience targeting remained accurate.

---

### Primary Marketing Mistake

Poor landing page experience combined with inconsistent messaging.

---

### Final Verdict

Although the advertisements successfully attracted visitors, the landing page failed to deliver the promised experience. Slow performance and inconsistent messaging caused users to leave before converting.

---

# Key Learnings

- Marketing decisions should always be supported by data.
- High traffic does not guarantee conversions.
- Customer experience after the click is equally important.
- Landing page optimization significantly impacts ROI.
- Message consistency across all channels improves trust.
- Evidence-based marketing leads to better campaign optimization.
- Customer behavior metrics reveal hidden campaign problems.
- Continuous testing and iteration are essential for marketing success.
