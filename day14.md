# Day 14 – AI Job Red Flag Detector

## 📌 Overview

This project is part of my **60-Day Claude AI Mastery Challenge**.

On Day 14, I explored how Claude AI can analyze a Job Description (JD) and company information to identify hiring risks, highlight positive signals, and generate interview questions.

## Deliverables

* ✅ Risk Analysis Report
* ✅ Screenshots of the generated report
* ✅ AI-generated Interview Questions
* ✅ Key Learnings

## Workflow

1. Set Claude Effort Level to Low.
2. Gather a Job Description.
3. Collect company information.
4. Paste the Job Red Flag Detector prompt.
5. Generate the analysis.
6. Review:

   * Overall Risk Score
   * Red Flags
   * Positive Signals
   * Risk Breakdown
   * Final Verdict
   * Interview Questions
7. Save screenshots and document the findings.

## Key Learning

Using AI for career intelligence can make company research more efficient, surface potential concerns early, and improve interview preparation by generating role-specific questions.
